About Hazak Shuaibu

My Journey

I entered the crypto space in 2020, fueled by a passion for decentralized finance and on-chain analytics. With over 5 years of experience, I’ve developed expertise in DeFi strategies, tokenomics, and market narratives. I contributed to LukCapital from 2022 to 2024, researching protocols like Pendle, Ethena, and FlowX, and publishing insights on their Substack and @teamlukcapital. As an Analyst & Alpha Lead at WhaleMoves Traders from 2021 to 2023, I shared smart money plays with over 3,000 users via Discord and @wmtcrypto. In 2024, I founded CryptoEdge, creating beginner-friendly content on macro triggers and daily alpha, shared at @hazakzy.

My goal is to simplify complex blockchain data into actionable strategies, engaging communities through transparent, meme-infused breakdowns on X and TradingView.
